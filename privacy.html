Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 13, 2026

Overview

Orahood Custom Business Solutions respects the fact that business information can be sensitive. This policy explains what information may be collected through this website, how it is used, and how OCBS approaches project data.

Information Collected

The contact form may collect your name, business or organization name, email address, optional phone number, preferred contact method, selected service need, project details, and consent to be contacted. You may also contact OCBS directly by email or phone instead of using the form.

Form Delivery

Website form submissions are processed through Formspree, a third-party form delivery service. Do not send passwords, bank account details, protected health information, payment card numbers, or other highly sensitive records through the contact form.

How Information Is Used

Information submitted through the website is used to respond to your inquiry, understand the project need, schedule follow-up, and prepare an appropriate recommendation or proposal. OCBS does not sell website contact information.

Project Data

Project work is handled according to the scope and data handling expectations agreed to for that engagement. OCBS favors practical, local-first systems when that is simpler, safer, and more maintainable. Cloud tools may be recommended only when they create real workflow value.

Retention and Deletion

Contact and project inquiry information is retained only as long as reasonably needed for communication, project evaluation, business records, or legal obligations. You may request deletion of website inquiry information by contacting OCBS directly.

Privacy Contact

For privacy questions or deletion requests, email dalton@orahood.net or call 405-639-8796.

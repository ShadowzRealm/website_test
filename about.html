About Orahood Custom Business Solutions

Local roots. Practical solutions.

Orahood Custom Business Solutions helps small businesses, churches, nonprofits, and organizations work smarter with their data and systems. We bring a practical, hands-on approach to every project, delivering solutions that are clear, reliable, and built for the long run.

Dalton Orahood

Founder and consultant

I'm Dalton Orahood, founder of Orahood Custom Business Solutions. I help Oklahoma businesses, churches, and organizations make better decisions by turning scattered data into clear, practical information.

My background is in mathematics, business, data analytics, and process improvement. I've built dashboards, automated repetitive workflows, cleaned complex datasets, and developed reporting systems that save time and make day-to-day operations easier to manage.

OCBS was founded on a straightforward idea: small organizations deserve the same quality of reporting and business intelligence that larger companies enjoy, without the complexity or cost.

Based in Newcastle, Oklahoma, I proudly serve businesses, churches, and nonprofits across the state with practical consulting built on integrity, transparency, and long-term relationships.
